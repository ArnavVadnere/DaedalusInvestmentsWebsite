What We Do

Capricorn is an owner-managed investment business focused on private equity.

In 2017, our record of successful investments attracted a well-respected strategic shareholder in the Ellerine Group. Capricorn has acquired and advised on the acquisition of shares in a diverse portfolio of both private and public companies, contributing to a highly lauded investment track record.

Capricorn has played a key role in establishing and growing numerous businesses through our investments in asset management, lending, insurance, retail and industrial investment holding companies. We have strategically narrowed our focus to the financial services sector in recent years.

Our Philosophy

We invest in businesses led by people we believe in.

We believe in supporting talented people who demonstrate advanced sector expertise, pioneering thinking and a value set that aligns with our own.

Our slogan Partnership Investing speaks to our tradition of working hand-in-hand with the leaders of our investment businesses in a shared pursuit for greater value for all Capricorn stakeholders.

We insist that our family values of honesty, integrity and humility underpin every interaction that represents our organisation as a whole.

Core Values

  • Independence

    Without direct links to any unaffiliated outside group, we eliminate institutionalized conflicts of interest and pressures.

  • Client Focus

    We understand each client is different and believe in personalization for each one. We believe in a long-term approach and we believe in accommodating each client’s specific goals and needs.

  • Transparency

    We wish to have open conversations with clients about the plan of action we wish to take along with keeping them in the loop on any major decisions as it pertains to asset management.

  • Ethical Investing

    We wish to comprise any portfolio with companies creating positive impacts on society, rather than ones that profit off of individuals in an immoral way.
